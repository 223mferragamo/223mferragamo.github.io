Summer 2021

My First Job

This summer, I got my first ever job. I worked at Frogbridge Day Camp as a counselor. I worked as a counselor with one other person and we had a group of 4th grade boys. The camp lasted 8 weeks and about 8 long hours each day. I had lots of fun, met lots of people, got more experience, and made some money. Workin there helped me make many memories like basketball, color war, and the bus. Most of the kids were great kids and were always super fun to be around. I'm glad that I worked there this summer because it defiently helped me grow.

Vacation

I went to Lake George with my family this summer. We spent a week there and stayed at a resort. Our apartment was almost right on the beach there so we had a great view of the lake and surrounding mountains. We did lots of fun things like white water rafting, train track biking, zipling, and more. I also went fishing on a boat with my dad. We caught lots of fish, but nothing too big. Somebody else on the boat caught a 20" bass, which was a Top 5 catch of the year. We go there every year and I'm excited to go back next year.

Friends

I saw my friends a lot this year. We went in the pool, played football, spikeball, and had lots of sleepovers. Our friend came down from New York for 2 weeks as well. I only went to the beach once this year, despite going almost every day last year.
